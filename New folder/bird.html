This bird has a call that sounds like "go-away". It has grey plumage, a white belly, and a huge crest on the top of its head. It also has white marks on its tail, which are visible when the bird is in flight.

This bird lives in the arid savannah and woodlands of Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Sudan, Somalia, and Ethopia. The IUCN lists this bird as least concern, with no major threat to its survival.
